All About Me


Emma-Blake was born in 2000 and is a proud native of Greenville, North Carolina. Her parents, Christine and Stephen Byrum, and younger sister Spencer now reside in Charlotte, NC. She is an alumna of Junius H. Rose High School and is a current undergraduate student at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. At UNC she is pursuing a degree in Journalism in the Hussman School of Journalism and Media, as well as a degree in Sports Administration through the Exercise and Sports Science department.
